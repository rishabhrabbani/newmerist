About MERIST Trust

MERIST - Multipurpose Educational and Rural Integrated Social Trust is a registered non-profit organization established in 2025 under The Indian Trusts Act, 1882. Based in Nellikuppam, Cuddalore District, Tamil Nadu, we are dedicated to empowering communities through education, social welfare, and rural development.

Our trust operates with complete transparency and accountability, ensuring every contribution makes a meaningful impact in the lives of those we serve.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: A society where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth regardless of their socio-economic background.

Mission: To provide multipurpose educational support, rural integrated development, and social welfare services to underprivileged communities in Tamil Nadu and beyond.

Working Sectors

  • Education & Literacy - School supplies, scholarships, literacy campaigns
  • Children Welfare - Nutrition programs, child development initiatives
  • Health & Family Welfare - Medical camps, health awareness drives
  • Rural Development - Infrastructure support, village development projects
  • Information & Communication Technology - Digital literacy, computer training
  • Women Empowerment - Skill training, self-help groups

How We Work

1. Identify: We identify communities and individuals in need through ground-level surveys and local partnerships.

2. Plan: We design targeted programs addressing specific needs - education, health, infrastructure, or skill development.

3. Execute: Our dedicated team implements programs with full transparency and community participation.

4. Impact: We measure outcomes and continuously improve our approach to maximize positive change.
